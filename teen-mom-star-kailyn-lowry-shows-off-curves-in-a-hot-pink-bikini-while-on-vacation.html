TEEN Mom 2 star Kailyn Lowry showed off her curves in a hot pink bikini.

She is currently on vacation with co-star Leah Messer.

Leah, 29, took to her Instagram Story on Wednesday to share videos of her and Kailyn, 29, spending quality time together as they soaked in the sun.

In one clip, Leah zoomed in on the mother of four as she rocked a hot pink bikini.

The Teen Mom 2 star’s hair was pulled back in a high bun, while she wore sunglasses and an orange life jacket and she held a smaller blue one in her hand.

Kailyn told Leah: “My feet are burning hot.”

The MTV stars have been documenting their fun trip on social media, as Leah revealed on Tuesday that she was finally able to meet Kailyn’s 11-month-old son Creed.

The mother of three happily held Kailyn’s youngest son in one snap, which she captioned: “Finally got to meet this sweet little guy.”

Leah added a sticker that read “obsessed” over the caption, while she tagged Kailyn and Creed’s Instagram accounts.

HAVING FUN

The friends continued to document their trip to the Dominican Republic, as Kailyn shared a clip of Leah braiding the mother of four’s hair.

Kailyn laughed as Leah told the camera: “Your hair did not get brushed, so don’t just me if there’s a lot of bumps in your braid.”

Creed’s mom then added: “Girl mom said I didn’t brush my hair.”

The MTV star then pointed out the baby hairs at front of her head, stating she hopes its “regrowth from having Creed.”

Leah continued to show off Creed on her Instagram Story as she re-shared a boomerang video from the Teen Mom Instagram account that showed her lifting the 11-month-old into the air.

While the caption explained Leah and Creed got to meet for the first time, the mother of three added four heart-eye emojis onto the post.

Kailyn shares her two youngest sons with ex Chris Lopez, while she is also the mother to 11-year-old son Isaac with ex Jo Rivera and seven-year-old son Lincoln with ex-husband Javi Marroquin.

Meanwhile, Leah shares 11-year-old twin daughters Aliannah and Aleeah with ex-husband Corey Simms and eight-year-old daughter Adalynn with ex-husband Jeremy Calvert.

SUPPORTIVE FRIENDS
