How did TobyMac start his Professional Career?

TobyMac started his professional music career by forming the Christian rock group “DC Talk” with Michael Tait, who was subsequently joined by Kevin Max Smith.

The group has so far put out five studio albums, all of which have won several awards. The entire band announced their dissolution to take a sabbatical and go solo around the year 2000.

With the release of his first studio album, “Momentum,” which debuted at the top of the charts, TobyMac’s solo career got underway.
