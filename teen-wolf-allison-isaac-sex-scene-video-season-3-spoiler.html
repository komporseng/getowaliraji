Teen Wolf Preview Clip: Allison &amp; Isaac Turn Up The Heat In January

June 01, 2024

The 'Teen Wolf' cast revealed major spoilers on MTV's 'Revelations' special -- but it's the Isaac/Allison preview clip that really blew our minds. Regardless of how you feel about Allison (Crystal Reed) and Isaac (Daniel Sharman) getting together on Teen Wolf, there's no denying that the forbidden couple will be providing fans with some much-needed heat this winter. Following the MTV hit's mid-season finale Aug. 19, the network teased us with a scene from the Jan. 6 return -- and it's equal parts sexy and scary.

In a nutshell, Allison is seen hooking up with Isaac — and loving it, I might add. She asks Isaac if he thinks Scott (Tyler Posey) is OK with them being together, and he assures her that the True Alpha doesn’t mind. Clothes come off, things get hotter… then all of a sudden, s**t gets real. The ghost of Kate Argent — that is Kate, right? — slithers out from behind Allison’s man-candy and starts choking him!

Allison wakes up — yes, it was merely a nightmare — but she still has a knife hidden underneath her. Is she going to screw Isaac… to death?

Scott & Allison: Both Moving On In 2014?

Crystal and Daniel briefly addressed Allison and Isaac’s future during Teen Wolf: Revelations, spelling out the characters’ feelings in black and white.

“She’s gorgeous, so of course … being Isaac, he’s extremely enamored by that,” Daniel said in the most charming British accent to ever grace God’s green earth. “[Their relationship is] not just thrown together. … There’s this feeling of ‘I don’t mean to feel this, I just do.'”

As for whether or not Allison would be jealous if Scott happens to find himself a new leading lady, Crystal’s pretty sure she’d play it cool.

“She wouldn’t be jealous,” Crystal said. “She would be genuinely happy for [Scott].”
