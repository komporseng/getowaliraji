The Top 10 Best UFC Events of All-Time

September 11, 2024
blog

The Ultimate Fighting Championship (UFC) has been home to some of the most iconic and thrilling events in the history of mixed martial arts.

From legendary title fights to intense rivalries and unforgettable moments, these events have left an indelible mark on the sport and its fans.

1. UFC 40: Vendetta (Nov. 22, 2002)

Venue: MGM Grand Garden Arena, Las Vegas
Main Event: Tito Ortiz TKO3 Ken Shamrock, defends light heavyweight title
Why it Mattered: The bad blood between Ortiz and Shamrock, along with the sizzle-reel knockout of Renato "Babalu" Sobral by Chuck Liddell, made this event significant. It also marked the debut of Joe Rogan on color commentary. This event's entertainment factor, financial success, and role in the development of the UFC make it a part of the top 10 list.

2. UFC 31: Locked and Loaded (May 4, 2001)

Venue: Trump Taj Mahal, Atlantic City, New Jersey
Main Event: Randy Couture UD Pedro Rizzo, defends heavyweight title
Why it Mattered: This event, the first with modern-day weight classes, showcased the rise of several UFC champions and the emergence of notable fighters. The Couture-Rizzo fight was considered the fight of the year in 2001, and Chuck Liddell recorded a first-round KO over Kevin Randleman.

3. UFC 194 (Dec. 12, 2015)

Venue: MGM Grand Garden Arena, Las Vegas
Main Event: Conor McGregor KO1 Jose Aldo, unifies featherweight titles
Why it Mattered: This event marked the night "Mystic Mac" became a living legend, predicting his 13-second KO of Aldo. The financial success, McGregor's historic victory, and the electric atmosphere make UFC 194 an iconic event in UFC history.

4. UFC 1 (November 12, 1993)

Venue: McNichols Arena, Denver
Main Event: Royce Gracie SUB Gerard Gordeau to win eight-man tournament
Why it Mattered: UFC 1 was a groundbreaking event that changed combat sports forever. It put Gracie Jiu-Jitsu and the UFC on the map, showcasing the effectiveness of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu in mixed martial arts.

5. UFC 100 (July 11, 2009)

Venue: Mandalay Bay Events Center, Las Vegas
Main Event: Brock Lesnar TKO2 Frank Mir, defends heavyweight title
Why it Mattered: UFC 100 is still regarded as one of the most iconic UFC events, with Lesnar's mauling of Mir and other compelling fights. It is remembered for its historical significance and the legendary performances of the fighters involved.

6. UFC 121

Significance: This event showcased the changing of the guard with Cain Velasquez dominating Brock Lesnar, marking a significant moment in UFC history.

7. UFC 116

Significance: This event featured memorable fights including the incredible Sexyama vs. Leben fight and Carwin vs. Lesnar, making it an iconic UFC event.

8. UFC 236

Significance: This event featured two incredible Fight of the Year contenders back-to-back in Adesanya vs. Gastelum and Poirier vs. Holloway, solidifying its place among the best UFC events.

9. UFC 261

Significance: UFC 261 was marked by 5 early finishes, including iconic knockouts and dominant performances, making it a phenomenal event in UFC history.

10. UFC 217

Significance: UFC 217 is hailed for its star power, entertainment, and historical impact, with iconic fights like Rose vs Joanna and GSP's return, making it a standout event in UFC history.
