Robert Parsons, better known as Bob Parsons, is an American entrepreneur. He is the founder of the Go Daddy group of companies, including domain name registrar GoDaddy.com, reseller registrar Wild West Domains and Blue Razor Domains. Other affiliated companies include Domains by Proxy, a domain privacy company, and Starfield Technologies, the business's technology development arm. As of September 2013, he has an estimated worth of $1.9 billion.Parsons is also the CEO and founder of YAM Worldwide, which is home to his entrepreneurial ventures in the fields of powersports, golf, real estate and marketing.In 2012, Parsons and his wife Renee founded The Bob & Renee Parsons Foundation. ﻿﻿﻿﻿﻿
